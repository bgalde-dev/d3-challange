Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

In regards to Household income there is a downward trend for all health risks as income increases

For age the risks are different. Obesity seems to have a downward trend as age increases, but is inconclusive with out further analysis. Smoking has an upward trend, and the percentage without healthcare decreases as age increases.

For all health risks in the below poverty have an upward trend.
